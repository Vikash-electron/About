Property Based 3D Molecule Generation

M.Sc. Project, IIT Bombay

  • Applied the method to generate molecules based on properties like polarizability, homo-lumo gap, heat capacity, etc., and evaluated the generated molecules regarding chemical validity, diversity, and uniqueness.
  • Further trying to generate the product of an elementary reaction by modifying the model to input multiple molecules instead of one and changing the objective function respectively.
